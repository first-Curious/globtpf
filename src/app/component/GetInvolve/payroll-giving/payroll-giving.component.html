What is Payroll Giving?

Payroll Giving is a charitable giving program that allows employees to contribute a portion of their salary or wages for the social cause of their choice. This contribution is typically deducted automatically from the employee's paycheck. The employer facilitates this process, making it a convenient and efficient way for employees to support charitable causes. Small, regular donations add up to significant support. Your monthly contributions, no matter the size, ensure a steady stream of assistance to those in need.
Payroll Giving provides employees with a convenient, tax benefits, and meaningful way to contribute to charitable causes, aligning their personal values with their workplace and fostering a sense of community and social responsibility.
